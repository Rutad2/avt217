The Blonde Abroad — Local’s Guide to Boston

URL of the site

https://www.theblondeabroad.com/locals-guide-to-boston/

Navigation

The site uses a simple blog layout. The header has a clear menu and search bar. Inside the article, subheadings make it easy to jump between sections. A footer repeats important links.

Usability

The guide is easy to read. Content is broken into short paragraphs with images that make it easier to follow along. Links like “Read More” or “Book Here” are straightforward. On mobile, it works well but long scrolls can feel tiring.

Typography and hierarchy

Headings are large and stand out, while body text is comfortable to read. Subheads and bold text create clear breaks between topics. There is good spacing between sections to help scanning.

Overall style/look and feel

The site feels friendly and personal, with warm travel photos and casual writing. The layout is clean and familiar for a blog, making it approachable for first‑time visitors.
