Why contrast matters

Typography is like music: harmony and contrast guide the rhythm and what we notice first. Below are seven simple ways to create contrast using only text, spacing, and color.

Source adapted for class demonstration.

1) Contrast of Size

Make an important thing bigger so the eye finds it first.
